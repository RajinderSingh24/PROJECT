Beginner Workout Plan

  • Monday: 30 minutes of cardio
  • Tuesday: Full body strength training
  • Wednesday: Rest day
  • Thursday: 30 minutes of cardio
  • Friday: Core workout
  • Saturday: Rest day
  • Sunday: Outdoor activity (e.g., hiking, cycling)

Intermediate Workout Plan

  • Monday: 40 minutes of cardio
  • Tuesday: Upper body strength training
  • Wednesday: Rest day
  • Thursday: 40 minutes of cardio
  • Friday: Lower body strength training
  • Saturday: Core workout
  • Sunday: Active recovery (e.g., yoga)

Advanced Workout Plan

  • Monday: 45 minutes of high-intensity interval training (HIIT)
  • Tuesday: Upper body strength training with focus on compound exercises
  • Wednesday: Rest day
  • Thursday: 45 minutes of cardio (running, cycling, or swimming)
  • Friday: Lower body strength training with emphasis on plyometrics
  • Saturday: Core workout and flexibility training
  • Sunday: Active recovery or rest day
